Chicken parmesan

Ingredients:

  1. 4 Chicken breasts
  2. Penne noodles
  3. 2 cups of bread crumbs
  4. 2 eggs
  5. 4-6 large tomatos
  6. 2-3 Garlic cloves
  7. 1 small onion
  8. 1 cup Olive oil
  9. Peper and salt
  10. Freash basil
  11. Mozzarella cheese
  12. Parmesan cheese
  13. 1/2 cup All purpose flour

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. If your using large chicken breats slice in half the long way. Lightly season chicken with salt and pepper.

Beat eggs in bowl. Mix bread crumbs and 1/4 cup of parmesan in seprate bowl, place aside. Place flour in bowl and coat chicken breasts. Dip floured breasts in eggs and then bread crumbs, set aside.

Heat olive oil in large skillet on medium heat until it starts to let off heat. Place breaded chicken in oil and cook each side for 2-4 minutes or until golden brown. Place chicken on baking dish and place enough tomato sause to cover top of chicken. Then place mozzarella cheese, parmasen chese and basil on top.

Bake in oven until cheese is bubbing and chicken is more browned and no longer pink in center,10-15 minutes.

Homemade pasta sauce with sausage and pene noodles

Ingrediants

  1. Sausages
  2. Penne noodles
  3. 4-6 large tomatos(needed for 4-6 pieces of chicken)
  4. tomato paste
  5. 2-3 Garlic cloves
  6. 1 small onion
  7. olive oil
  8. Basil
  9. parmesan cheese
  10. peper and salt

Follow instructions for making the pene noodles from the box. Dice tomatoes, onion, and garlic and place aside.

Pour 1/4 cup olive oil in sause pan and let it heat up. Then puor the garlic and onion in the heated oil and let it slightly brown. Add tomatos and stir occassionally. Let tomatos soften and let the skin and tomato turn to sause. Add in tomato paste and stir.

If too thick add a small amount of water and stir. If to liquidy add more tomatos or paste. Make sure not to add too much water or the sause will be too liquidy.
